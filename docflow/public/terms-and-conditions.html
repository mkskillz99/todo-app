Program Name

DocFlow by MKS Ventures — SMS Receipt and Materials Tracking Service

Program Description

DocFlow is an SMS-based service that allows construction crew employees of Gar Rector and Associates, Inc. to text photos of material purchase receipts for automated job cost tracking. The service confirms receipt, logs materials to the correct construction project, and notifies project managers for review.
