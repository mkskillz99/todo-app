Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how MKS Ventures, LLC ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information through DocFlow, our SMS-based receipt and materials tracking service operated in connection with Gar Rector and Associates, Inc.

Information We Collect

When you use DocFlow, we may collect the following information:

  • Your mobile phone number
  • Photos of material purchase receipts that you text to our service number
  • Receipt data extracted from those photos, including vendor name, purchase amount, item descriptions, and date of purchase
  • Job and project assignment information associated with your purchases
  • Message content sent to and from our service number

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To log material purchases to the correct construction project for job cost tracking
  • To send confirmation messages back to you acknowledging receipt of your submission
  • To notify project managers of material purchases for review and approval
  • To generate cost reports and job summaries for project management
  • To improve the accuracy and functionality of our service

Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Your information may be shared only with:

  • Your employer (Gar Rector and Associates, Inc.) for job cost tracking and project management purposes
  • Service providers necessary to operate DocFlow, including Twilio (messaging), and cloud hosting providers

Data Retention

We retain receipt data and associated records for the duration of the construction project plus any period required for tax, accounting, or legal compliance. Phone numbers and message logs are retained for as long as you are an active user of the service.

Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. Data is transmitted over encrypted connections and stored in secure cloud infrastructure.

Your Rights and Choices

You may opt out of receiving messages at any time by replying STOP to any message from our service number. You may request deletion of your data by contacting us using the information below. You may also text HELP to our service number for assistance.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you via SMS or other reasonable means. Your continued use of the service after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
